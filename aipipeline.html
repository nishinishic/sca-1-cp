- Each uploaded photo will be analyzed and described by an AI image recognition model in Ground Image classifications.

- A Satellite Image will be identified based on the longitude and latitude of the captured Ground Image.
Satellite Image tags will be produced by satellite image recognition model (TensorFlow) and described in Satellite Image classificationsxw.

- The Ground Image classification tags and the Satellite Image classification tags will be combined to produce and generate new AI-generated Satellite Exchange Image.
